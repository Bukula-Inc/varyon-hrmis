Work Information

Job Description

Responsible for developing, testing, and maintaining software applications that meet the needs of the organization.

Responsibilities
  • Develop and implement new software programs.
  • Maintain and improve the performance of existing software.
  • Collaborate with other departments to define project scope and deliverables.
  • Provide technical support and training to end-users.
Goals and Objectives

Increase code efficiency by 20% within the next quarter, and successfully deploy the upcoming project on time and within budget.

Performance Metrics (KPIs)
  • Code quality score: 90% or higher.
  • Project completion rate: 95% on time.
  • Customer satisfaction rating: 4.5/5 or higher.
  • Number of bugs reported: less than 10 per project.
Work Schedule

Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Occasional overtime may be required during project deadlines.
